Grammarly and Style Guides
==========================

While Sphinx handles structure, **Grammarly** ensures that the prose remains professional, concise, and free of grammatical errors.

Maintaining Tone of Voice
-------------------------

Our documentation follows a **helpful, direct, and objective** tone. Grammarly helps enforce this by flagging:

* **Passive Voice:** Converting to active voice for clearer instructions.
* **Wordiness:** Removing unnecessary fillers to improve scannability.
* **Consistency:** Identifying variations in spelling (e.g., American vs. British English).

.. important::
   Grammarly is a supplement, not a replacement for a human editor. It may not 
   understand highly specific technical jargon or domain-specific context.

Workflow Integration
--------------------

We recommend the following workflow:

1.  Draft content in ``.rst`` files.
2.  Run a final check using the **Grammarly Browser Extension** or Desktop App.
3.  Apply suggestions that align with our internal **Style Guide**.
